What changed (summary of the priority fixes)

1. One primary flow

Textarea is the hero. "Run full analysis" and "Compare latest vs previous" become presets that fill the input. Suggestion chips moved above the input.

2. Context line removed

The dense "11 reports · 74 biomarkers · Conventional · Test E…" string is gone. Users have no control over those values, so showing them adds noise without adding a decision.

3. Explicit usage meter

"0/5 today · 1/25 month" replaced with labeled progress bars and a "What's this?" affordance.

4. Grouped sidebar

Nine flat items reorganized into Your data / Protocols / AI. Upload PDF demoted to a compact button in the sidebar footer.

5. Less label redundancy

"Ask AI" no longer appears as page + section + button. Page = AI Coach, section = Your question, button = Ask AI.
